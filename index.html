RuneMod

What is RuneMod??
RuneMod is a plugin that can create, share and download runescape mods.

How does it work?
RuneMod works by sending data from the runescape client to unreal engine via a client plugin.

RuneMod consists of two parts. One part is the runelite plugin: this is currently open source.

The other part is an application made with unreal engine. Atleast for now, this part is not open Source.
